Defense Ranking

The defense ranking measures how effectively a team plays defense against their opponents.

How It's Calculated

For each match where a team plays defense:

  1. Calculate the EPA of the opposing alliance
  2. Compare this to the actual score the opposing alliance achieved in the match
  3. The difference is the defense impact for that match
  4. The defense ranking is based on the average impact across all matches
  5. If an opponent robot died in the match, the robot's defense isn't taken into account

Interpreting the Numbers

  • Higher positive numbers mean the team is more effective at defense
  • Negative numbers suggest the opposing alliance scored more than expected
  • Rank shows how the team compares to others who play defense
